Project Planning

When a client came to us and asked if we can assist them with planning a project to put forward to management we was happy to help. We held a number of conference meetings with the client to understand their vision and how they wanted the system to work. We then constructed a project plan and outlined the user stories and goals of the project along with detailed information about the project for management to evaluate.

Bespoke Dashboard

We was recently comissioned by a large long term customer to build a bespoke dashboard and control panel to allow them to manage their customers and report on the data. For this we built them a bespoke client management system. Being a bespoke system, this allowed the client flexibility in the goals and requirements of the system and enabled them to have more features than they originally required and a more powerful control panel.
